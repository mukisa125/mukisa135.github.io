About

I am Nakanyike Maria 17 years old born in 2006 ,I took interest in the chess career in 2013 after my brothers emerged victors in the chess tournament. By that time I was a pupil at St.Marcelino Primary School.

My father Mr. Augustin started training me to play chess and also gave me to read books written by grand masters of chess to improve on my talent. Quick thought and deep decision making driver me to success.                     Chess improved my understanding of maths and English at school.
